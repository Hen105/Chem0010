MATTER AND ENERGY

Objectives

On completion of the unit you should be able to:

  1. distinguish between physical and chemical properties.
  2. define melting point, boiling point and solubility.
  3. distinguish between physical and chemical changes.
  4. sketch out a classification scheme for matter.
  5. define and give examples of elements and compounds.
  6. define and give examples of pure substances, homogeneous mixtures and heterogeneous mixtures.
  7. define potential and kinetic energy.
